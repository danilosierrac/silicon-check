# Silicon Check

**Will your stuff survive macOS 28?**

Silicon Check scans your Mac for Intel-only apps **and plug-ins** — including the audio (AU/VST), QuickLook, Spotlight, color picker and printer plug-ins that Apple's built-in checker misses — and tells you exactly what will stop working when macOS 28 drops Intel support.

Free on the Mac App Store. No accounts, no data collection, read-only.
