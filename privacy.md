# Privacy Policy — Silicon Check

**Silicon Check does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Ever.**

- All scanning happens locally on your Mac. The contents of your disk never leave your machine.
- The app reads files only in locations you allow, with read-only access, inside the macOS App Sandbox.
- The only network request the app makes is an anonymous query to Apple's public iTunes Search API (itunes.apple.com) to check whether an updated version of an Intel-only app exists on the App Store. This request contains the app's bundle identifier and your country's storefront code — nothing about you or your device.
- No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no third-party SDKs.

The optional "Supporter Tip" purchase is processed entirely by Apple; Silicon Check never sees your payment information.
